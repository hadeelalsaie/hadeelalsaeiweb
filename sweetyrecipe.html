Chocolate Cupcakes with Chocolate Frosting

Amount: 12 Cupcakes
Serving: 6-8 people
Total Time: 2 hours

Ingredients:

1.5 cups of all-purpose flour
1 cup of sugar
1/3 cup of cocoa powder
1 teaspoon of baking soda
1/2 teaspoon of salt
1 cup of coffee
1 tablespoon of white vinegar
1/3 cup vegan oil
1 big egg
2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
1 cup of unsalted butter
3 cups of confectioner sugar
1/2 cup of cocoa powder
3 tablespoons of heavy whipping cream
2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
1/4 teaspoon of salt

Tools:

-Whisker
-Measuring cups
-Electric hand mixture
-Piping bag
-Decorating tip
-Muffin pan

Recipe:

Before Starting you have to preheat the oven to 175 Celsius degrees.

First create the first mixture by combining the dry ingredients, which are 1.5 cups of all-purpose flour 1 cup of sugar, 1/3 cup of cocoa powder, 1 teaspoon of baking soda, ½ teaspoon of salt and whisk them to break any clumps and leave this mixture aside.

Next, combine the wet ingredients by adding 1 cup of coffee, 1 tablespoon of white vinegar, 1/3 cup vegan oil, 1 big egg and 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract and beat them together.

Then, add the wet ingredients mixture to the try one and use the hand whisker to combine everything together and make sure you don’t over mix, because we don’t want to develop the gluten in the batter.

Later, line up the cupcake papers in a cupcake tray, fill each liner to 2/3 full and then let them bake inside the middle rack of the oven for about 20 minutes.

Before taking them out make sure that when you insert a toothpick comes out clean, let it cool until it reaches room temperature and meanwhile prepare the buttercream frosting.

For the frosting you need to beat one cup of unsalted butter at a high speed for 3 minutes with an electric hand mixer until the butter becomes light in color, then add 3 cups of confectioner sugar, ½ cup of coca powder, 3 table spoons of heavy whipping cream and 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract, and beat the frosting again, first beat in a low speed then switch to a medium speed and keep whisking for more 3 minutes.

Then, transfer the frosting into a piping bag and add any type of tip you like to decorate the cupcake with the frosting.

Finally, the cupcakes are ready to be served and enjoyed.
