About Me

My name is Hadeel Alsaei, and I started baking early in life when I was helping my mom bake cakes for our family gatherings.
Later on, the love of baking grew in my heart and became my favorite thing to do in my free time.
And because I consider the time that I spend baking as therapy, I created this website because I would love to see everyone experience this joy.
