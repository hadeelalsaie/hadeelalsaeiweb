Tomato and Cheese Pastries

Amount: 12 Puff Pastries
Serving: 4-6 people
Total Time: 1 hour

Ingredients:

-One pack of puff pastry squares

-Tomato pesto

-100 grams of mozzarella cheese

-100 grams of cheddar cheese

-100 grams of feta cheese

-Two big tomatoes

-One pack of cherry tomatoes

-Oregano

-One egg

Recipe:

First preheat the oven to 180 Celsius degrees, and line the baking pan with packing paper to avoid the pastries sticking to the pan.

Second slice each cheese into 12 slices and slice the two tomatoes each to 6 slices. Then stretch the corners, spread a teaspoon of the tomato presto in the middle of the puff pastry square, add a slice of mozzarella, cheddar, and a slice of feta cheese and on top add a slice of tomato and sprinkle some oregano, to secure the pastry bring two corners of the square on top of each other.

For decoration, put one cherry tomato on top and sprinkle some oregano.

Finally, brush the pastry with the egg and bake for 30 minutes and serve it warm for breakfast.

Tools:

Baking pan

Knife
